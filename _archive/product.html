The Aura Vest represents the pinnacle of structural macramé. Hand-knotted in our Sofia atelier using 100% sustainable organic cotton cord, this piece effortlessly bridges the gap between heritage craft and contemporary avant-garde silhouette.

Material & Care +

Crafted from 100% organic, sustainably sourced cotton cord. Spot clean only with a damp, light-colored cloth. Do not machine wash, bleach, or tumble dry. Store flat to maintain structural integrity.

Shipping & Returns +
